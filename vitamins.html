💎VIT A(RETINOL)-👁️ Animal foods like milk,butter,ghee,eggs,fish are richest source of vitamin A
Plant foods. Contains of BETA-CAROTENE (precursor of Vitamin A. Ripe fruit s like mango,papaya and vegetables like carrots🥕 and pumpkin are richest source of BETA-CAROTENE.
Functions--😍
1. Maintaing normal vision
2. Supporting growth
3. Protecting against disease like respiratory
💎VITAMIN D/SUNSHINE VITAMIN💪🏻
Sources:- Sunlight & foods of animal origin like eggs,liver,butter and fish liver oils.
Functions😍-
1.Makes bones strong.
2.Helps in absorption of calcium and phosphorus.
💎VITAMIN E🙃
All foodstuffs contain vitamin E.
Good sources are whole grains,🥦dark green leafy vegetables,nuts and pulses
Food sources of ANIMAL ORIGIN are LOW in Vitamin E.
Functions😍-
Main role in our body of vitamin E is protection to other Vitamin s like A and C.
💎VITAMIN K🥦/antibleeding vitamin❣️-
Green leafy vegetables like spinach,🥗cabbage,lettuce are richest source of vitamin K
Animal foods like egg yolk,milk are good sources of vitamin K
Functions😍-
It prevents uncontrolled bleeding❤
